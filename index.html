Swooping Eagle Travel and Tours

We are a Manila Travel Agency whose head office is in Parañaque City. we started operating in February of 2018. Since our humble beginnings we have helped hundreds of satisfied Philippines tourists from over 50 countries around the world. We are now one of the world's most popular travel and tourism agency in the Philippines.

We, the founders of Swooping Eagle Travel and Tours Agency, Inc., are a group of college students who are dedicated in providing quality customer experiences for travel and tourism. We believe that through great service we can help our clients have an amazing and meaningful experience in the Philippines.
